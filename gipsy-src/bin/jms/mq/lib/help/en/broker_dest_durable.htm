Broker Destination Properties >
Durable Subscriptions

This tab displays a list of the durable subscribers to the current topic destination.

For each subscriber, the table specifies the Client ID of the subscriber, the number of messages awaiting to be consumed, and the state of the durable subscription.

To delete a durable subscriber:

  1. Click on the subscriber in the table to select that subscriber.
  2. Click the Delete button. Note that this deletion is immediate - you do not have to click on the OK button.

To purge messages for a subscriber:

  1. Click on the subscriber in the table to select that subscriber.
  2. Click the Purge button. Note that this action is immediate - you do not have to click on the OK button.
